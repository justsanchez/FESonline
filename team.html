Javier Morales

Co President

Originally from Durham, North Carolina, with Mexican roots. He is a devoted and service-oriented individual aspiring to make a lasting impact through technology. Pursuing a major in computer science with a software engineering concentration and a minor in cybersecurity, he aims for a career in full-stack development. His leadership and passion for growth are evident in roles like co-founder of 'Futuros en STEM' and co-president of the Hispanic Latinx Student Union. Javier, a recipient of the LSAMP scholarship, is driving innovation at the intersection of language and technology, showcasing commitment to excellence, leadership, and knowledge advancement.
