Empowering Latinos in STEM

We are a passionate team of dedicated individuals, united by a common goal: to empower and uplift Latinos in STEM

Join us to support Latinos in STEM. Together, we empower diversity and drive progress

Futuros En Stem dedicated to supporting and empowering Latinos professionals in STEM. Through mentorship, safe spaces, advocacy, and outreach, we're building a community that celebrates diversity, cultural heritage, and innovation

Fueled by a deep commitment, our organization strives to uplift and empower Latinos individuals in science, technology, engineering, and math. We focus on fostering a tight-knit community that promotes inclusivity, advocates for growth, and celebrates diversity

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I Join FES?

    To join Futuros en STEM (FES), please check our social media outlet for volunteer/workshop opportunities or contact us directly for more information on joining as a participant or volunteer.
    ^

  • Who benefits from Futuros en STEM programs?

    Futuros en STEM programs benefit students interested in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) fields, fostering their educational and career development.
    ^

  • What impact has Futuros en STEM had on the community since its establishment?

    Futuros en STEM has significantly increased STEM interest among rising community of underrepresented STEM UNCW students, leading to a 30% rise in student participation in STEM interest and a notable improvement in pursuement in STEM fields.
    ^

  • How can I stay updated on Futuros en STEM's latest news and events?

    You can stay updated on Futuros en STEM's latest news and events by following our social media accounts and checking our official website regularly.
